Handling: In a dystopian future, the totalitarian nation of Panem is divided between 12 districts and the Capitol. The 24 participants are forced to eliminate their competitors while the citizens of Panem are required to watch. When 16-year-old Katniss's young sister, Prim, is selected as District 12's female representative, Katniss volunteers to take her place.
